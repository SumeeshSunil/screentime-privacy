# Privacy Policy for Screen Time Blocker

**Last Updated: October 2023**

Screen Time Blocker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your app selections, timer settings, and usage statistics are stored locally on your device and never leave your phone.

## 2. Accessibility Service Usage
Our app uses the Android Accessibility Service API to provide its core function:
- **Purpose:** To detect when you open an app that you have previously chosen to "lock".
- **Operation:** When a locked app is detected, we redirect you to our lock screen to help you stay focused.
- **Privacy:** We **DO NOT** read your screen content, we **DO NOT** track your keystrokes, and we **DO NOT** collect any data from other apps.

## 3. Contact Us
If you have any questions, you can contact us via our GitHub repository.
